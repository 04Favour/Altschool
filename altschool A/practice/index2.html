Biography

Steve Jobs, the founder of apple companies was a very nice man. He always came to the rescue of his company anytime they needed him. He was a philanthropist supporting many charity quests and good gestures. He owns a foundation that looks after orphaned children. Unfortunately, he passed away to early in life. his good works will not be forgotten. Especially from those he has helped to start businesses and the likes. This biography is not a complete work of his doings, just a note of appreciation about him. He was a great man. he would not be forgotten in a hurry. Rest on great man.
