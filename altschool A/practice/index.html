Biography

Steven Paul Jobs(february 24, 1955 - october 5, 2011) was an American entrepreneur, industrial designer, business magnate, media proprietor, and investor. He was the co-founder, chairman, and ceo of apple; the chairman and the majority shareholdr of pixar; a member of the walt disney company's board of directors following its acquisition of pixar; and the founder, chairman, and the ceo of next. he is widely recognized as the pioneer of the personal computer revolution of the 1970s and the 1980s, along with his early business partner and fellow apple co-founder Steve Wozniak
